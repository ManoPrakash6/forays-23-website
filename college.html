Title
Title
A Young Theorem of Volterra
Abstract
Abstract
In this talk we shall explore the concept of continuity on R. Continuity is the most important concept in mathematics and applications of mathematics in science and engineering. We shall explore some examples of functions with strange continuity behavior. Motivated by this, we go into proving Volterra's Theorem on non existence of a function that is continuous at every rational and discontinuous at every irrational point in R.
